WHAT

IS

ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a language model that learns from human speech. The database that can be accessed is huge. Through complex algorithms and neural networks, it can provide an answer based on the database. It can base sentences on meaning because it can predict the sequence of words.
One of the problems that ChatGPT creates is that many online publications fire their authors and use it to generate texts for the articles.
